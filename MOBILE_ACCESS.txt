                    📱 HOW TO SCAN QR CODE FROM MOBILE
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

PROBLEM: QR code shows "site can't be reached" on mobile
REASON: You're using localhost, which only works on your computer

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
                            ✅ SOLUTION (2 OPTIONS)
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

┌─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│ OPTION 1: USE TUNNEL URL (Works from ANYWHERE - Recommended!)              │
└─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┘

STEP 1: Find Your Tunnel URL
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
Look at your terminal where "npm run dev:all" is running.
Find the line that says:

    [3] your url is: https://xxxxx.loca.lt

Copy that FULL URL (example: https://tight-pigs-dig.loca.lt)


STEP 2: Open App with Tunnel URL
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
1. Close any localhost tabs
2. Paste the tunnel URL in your browser
3. If you see "Click to Continue", click it
4. You'll see the chat app



STEP 3: Create Room
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
1. Click "Create Room"
2. You'll see a GREEN box saying "✅ GLOBAL ACCESS"
3. The QR code now works from ANY network!
4. Scan with your phone - IT WILL WORK! 🎉


┌─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│ OPTION 2: USE LOCAL NETWORK (Same WiFi Only)                               │
└─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┘

STEP 1: Make sure your phone is on the SAME WiFi as your computer

STEP 2: Open the app at http://localhost:4000

STEP 3: Create a room
- You'll see a BLUE box saying "📱 LOCAL NETWORK"
- The QR code will work for devices on your WiFi
